# Planting the Bay - Web Design Brief Summary

Original PDF: `Planting-the-Bay-Web-Design-Brief_1.pdf`

## Core objective

Build a fundraising, storytelling, and vision-casting website for a Bay Area church-planting initiative. The site should clearly explain what is happening, why the Bay Area matters, and how people can give or get involved.

## Primary conversion flows

Prioritize these two flows above everything else:

1. Storytelling - help visitors understand the mission quickly and emotionally.
2. Giving / getting involved - make donation, volunteer, staff, relocate, pray, and email-capture actions feel seamless.

## Design direction

- Modern, editorial, clean, confident, and contemporary.
- Mobile-first, because most traffic is expected from social.
- Video/photo-forward with real Bay Area faces and locations.
- Energetic but trustworthy for donors.
- Strong sense of Bay Area geography: campuses, bridges, neighborhoods, and region-by-region expansion.

## Main navigation

- Home
- Our Story
- Our Vision
- Why the Bay
- Get Involved
- Give

Footer / secondary pages:

- The Team
- Updates / Stories
- Prayer
- Partners
- FAQ
- Contact

## Homepage requirements

- Short vision-focused hero statement.
- Hero video or strong hero imagery.
- Primary CTAs: Give and Get Involved.
- Berkeley first -> expand across the Bay visual strip.
- Fundraising progress toward Year 1 goal.
- Why the Bay teaser.
- Featured story / latest updates teaser.
- Email capture.
- First-time visitors from Instagram should understand the whole story in one scroll.

## Key pages

### Our Story
Tell Stuart and Ashley's story, the SF Bay Fellowship partnership, and the heart behind the planting initiative. Use photos, pull quotes, and short video clips.

### Our Vision
Show the strategy: social media outreach, pop-up services, campus ministry growth, and the expansion roadmap: Berkeley -> San Francisco -> Peninsula -> San Jose -> Tri-Valley -> beyond.

### Why the Bay
Use stats, maps, and simple infographics to explain why the Bay Area is a high-leverage church planting opportunity.

### Get Involved
Create clear pathways:

- Join the team / planting staff
- Serve / volunteer
- Pray
- Relocate

Forms should be segmented by pathway and show clear next steps after submission.

### Give
Make this the strongest conversion page:

- One-time vs recurring toggle, with recurring emphasized.
- Suggested impact tiers.
- Named monthly sustainer program, such as Founding 100 or Bay Builders.
- Fund designation.
- Tax-deductibility and receipt note.
- Card and ACH minimum; DAF, stock, and major gifts can be contact links.
- Transparent budget / where your gift goes.
- Mobile giving should feel like a 2-3 tap flow.

## Functional requirements to plan for

- Donation platform integration.
- Fundraising progress meter.
- Events / pop-up service module with RSVP and map/location support.
- Multi-pathway forms.
- Email/newsletter capture.
- Blog/CMS.
- Interactive expansion map with the 3D Bay Area flyover asset.
- Social embeds and shareable story pages.

## Technical requirements

- Mobile-first and fully responsive.
- Fast-loading optimized images/video and lazy loading.
- Canonical domain: plantingthebay.com.
- Redirect plantthebay.com to plantingthebay.com.
- SEO metadata and Open Graph cards.
- Analytics and conversion tracking.
- Accessibility-conscious implementation.
- CMS-editable stories, progress meter, events, and team info.
- SSL, spam protection, and PCI-compliant giving.
