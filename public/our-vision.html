Our Vision

A multi-region movement across the Bay.

The strategy is simple to understand and big enough to require faith: digital outreach, pop-up services, campus ministry growth, and a roadmap from Berkeley outward.

Fund the missionJoin the team
Pillar 01

Social media outreach

Use digital reach to find and engage people across the Bay, then move conversations toward real relationships and community.

Pillar 02

Pop-up services

Host regional gatherings and RSVP moments so seekers, supporters, and relocating families can experience the vision in person.

Pillar 03

Campus ministry growth

Build on Bay Area campuses, beginning in Berkeley, with a model that can scale as the movement expands.

Expansion Roadmap

Berkeley → SF → Peninsula → San Jose → Tri-Valley → beyond.
