The Mission

A church-planting and campus-ministry movement beginning in Berkeley and expanding across the San Francisco Bay Area.

Get Involved

Three-year vision

Beginning September 1, Stuart and Ashley Mains are planting first in Berkeley with the SF Bay Fellowship, then expanding outward across the Bay.

Strategy includes:

  • Social media outreach
  • Pop-up services
  • Campus ministry growth
Get Involved
