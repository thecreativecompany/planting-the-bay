Berkeley First: The Launch Story

Share

Planting the Bay begins in Berkeley on September 1, in partnership with the SF Bay Fellowship, with a vision to expand across the entire Bay Area.

The first step is Berkeley: campus ministry and a local church plant working together so students, families, and seekers have a clear next step.

From there, the vision expands to San Francisco, the Peninsula, San Jose, the Tri-Valley, and beyond through social outreach, pop-up services, and campus ministry growth.

Supporters can give, pray, serve locally, relocate, or explore staff opportunities as the movement grows.
