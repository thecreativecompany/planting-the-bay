FAQ

Prepared for Planting the Bay

Where does the money go?

Funds support the Berkeley launch, campus ministry, staff couples, pop-up services, communications, and expansion preparation. Berkeley launches first in partnership with the SF Bay Fellowship, then expands across San Francisco, the Peninsula, San Jose, Tri-Valley, and beyond.

Can I give or serve?

You can give, serve, pray, relocate, or explore joining the staff team. Each pathway should lead to a clear next step and follow-up confirmation.
